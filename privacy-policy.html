Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

1. Overview

Just Spices (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Just Spices mobile app.

2. Information We Collect

Just Spices does not require account creation and does not collect personal identifying information such as your name, address, phone number, or payment details.

The app may store pantry-related data locally on your device (such as your spice inventory) for functionality purposes only.

3. Barcode Scanning

When you scan a barcode, Just Spices uses third-party product databases to identify the item. Barcode data may be transmitted temporarily to retrieve matching product information.

We do not sell, rent, or personally identify users through barcode scans.

4. Data Usage

Any pantry data stored in the app is used solely to provide the app’s features, such as tracking your spices and preventing duplicate purchases.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Just Spices is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.
